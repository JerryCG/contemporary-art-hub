Construction No.95 (Alexander Rodchenko, Constructivism)

Of the Rodchenko's hugely varied output, perhaps no work is more delicately poised between the shifting social and cultural forces that determined the artist's philosophy than his 1919 masterpiece, Construction No.95. Construction No.95 is part of a series of works Rodchenko built around a single geometric concept - the line - central to Constructivism, and with it, much of contemporary graphic design.

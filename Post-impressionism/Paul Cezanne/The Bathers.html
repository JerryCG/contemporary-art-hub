The Bathers (Paul Cezanne, Post-impressionism)

The Bathers is actually the largest version of a series of Bather paintings by Cezzane. It is ccasionally referred to as the Big Bathers or Large Bathers to distinguish it from the smaller works, the painting is considered one of the masterpieces of modern art, and is often considered Cezanne's finest work. The painting was featured in the BBC Two series 100 Great Paintings.

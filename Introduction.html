Introduction

Contemporary Art (Western) is a collective term that includes various art genres and art concepts happening in the European countries, the USA, especially in Paris (France) from the late 19th century to the middle/late of 20th century till now.

On the whole, contemporary art is an extremely complicated and unpredictable art phenomenon. No matter the forms of arts or the spirits hidden in arts, western contemporary goes beyond the asthetic category of western traditional art. It builds up a new system of diversified modern visual views and cultural traditions.

This website is designed to help people who are interested in contemporay art learn about it in an eaiser and more relaxing manner.

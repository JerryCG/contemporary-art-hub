The Two Barges (Andre Derain, Fauvism)

The Two Barges is the representative artwork of the artist Derain. Pure emerald Green, pansy and croci are boldy spread on the canvas. This kind of water scene is far more "impressive" than artworks of Impressionists. This painting has exaggerated colors and bold brush strokes, which are like colorful bombs exploding on the canvas.

Barge on the Seine near Le Pecq (Maurice de Vlaminck, Fauvism)

Jerry CG's comments: This painting is one of Vlaminck's famous Fauvist artworks. The nearby lawn is painted in orange and yellow, and water is pale and white, which shows the representative features of Fauvism. The brush strokes are bold and rough, but still create a harmonious picture, which gives viewers visual relaxation and mind peace.

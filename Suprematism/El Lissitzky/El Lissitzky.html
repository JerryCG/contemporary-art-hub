El Lissitzky (Russian, 1890-1941, Suprematism)

El Lissitzky was an avant-garde Russian artist and major figure in Suprematism. Lissitzky's entire career was laced with the belief that the artist could be an agent for change, later summarized with his edict, "goal-oriented creation".

Preliminary Sketch for a Poster (El Lissitzky, Suprematism)

Preliminary Sketch for a Poster was created by El Lissitzky in Suprematism style. It is composed of basic geometric shapes, creating a pure and ordered feeling which is typical of Suprematism concept.

Siegfried L. Kratochwil (Austrian, 1916-2005, Primitivism)

Siegfried Leopold Kratochwil was an Austrian painter and poet. Born in Karlstift, Lower Austria he later moved to Vienna and became one of the best-known Austrian Primitivism artists by the end of the 20th century.

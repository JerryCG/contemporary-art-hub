Jerry CG's Contemporary Art

a. Xerox Art

Xerox Art is the art of "Copy of Copy". By the method of "Degeneration", we can get pictures of different texture qualities.

In my work, first I took a picture of my hometown's beautiful watery scene with floating boats and ancient town. Then I made a black-and-white copy of the original photo. And I continued to make 8 copies sequentially. You could see when pictures are continuously copied, great amount of details will be lost and this will create special visual arts. Enjoy it!

b. Sound Map

Sound map is a kind of art that can visualize the sounds. We use different dots, lines, curves ans shapes to depict the volume, pitch, indensity of sounds. In my work, I recorded the sounds when I went from Festival Walk to Olympia City. Detailed explanations are shown in the sound map. Enjoy it!
